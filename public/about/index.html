WE ARE TRAVIS HIGH SCHOOL HABITAT FOR HUMANITY

Located at Richmond, TX, we are the Habitat For Humanity club as Travis High School. We do a variety of activities aimed towards assisting the needy, helping the environment, and making the world a better place.
